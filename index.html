Calvin Black Student Union

About Us

BSU is a student led organization that provides resources for students of African-descent and allies to close the educational gap on Calvin’s campus as well as the Grand Rapids area.

In order to achieve this goal, we unite and support Black students on campus.

We aim to be a platform for other organizations and groups that serve black students and our student body as a whole.

We host general assembly meetings, plan BSU sponsored events, and attend events on campus and in the community.

Our goal is to celebrate Black excellence by supporting and promoting individual achievements while also educating about the contributions of different ethnic and cultural heritages.

We spread awareness and actively fight against institutional and individual acts of racism.

Our Vision

We, as members of the Black Student Union,

desire to build unity among Black students at Calvin University by providing opportunities for Black students to explore, learn, and grow.

Our mission is to take our charge as Calvin students to think deeply, to act justly,
and to live wholeheartedly as Christ’s agents of renewal in the world.

We seek to cultivate a sense of community, solidarity, and excellence through engagement in our community at Calvin University,
the larger Grand Rapids area, and beyond.
